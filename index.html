The 13 Flows We're Building
🥾
Legendary Club Welcome Series
Trigger — New member joins as a Wanderer (Toki join event)
3–5 emails over 10 days. Opens with the legend arc — the path, the promise, what Staves are and how to earn them, what Keys and Collectibles unlock at each chapter. Drives the first bottle purchase before interest fades. Purchase behavior exits at every step. The entry point for the entire program.
Onboarding
⚗️
Stave Earned Confirmation
Trigger — Toki: member earns Staves (purchase, challenge, referral, social action)
Quick, satisfying confirmation that fires every time a member earns Staves. Shows their updated balance, tells them exactly how close they are to their next Key or Collectible, and names the chapter milestone that's within reach. Keeps the program feeling alive between purchases.
Points
⏳
Stave Balance Reminder
Trigger — Toki: member has unspent Staves and no activity in 30 days
A periodic nudge to members sitting on unspent Staves. Shows their exact balance, what they can redeem right now, and what they're X Staves away from unlocking next. Single CTA. No fluff. The goal is to pull passive earners back into the program loop before they forget they're in it.
Points
🔥
Stave Expiry Warning
Trigger — Toki: member's Staves are approaching expiry date
Urgency-forward single email that fires when Staves are close to expiring. Names the exact number at risk, the exact date they're gone, and gives one clear CTA to redeem before they're lost. This is the highest-open-rate transactional email in any loyalty program — people hate losing things they earned.
Points
🔑
Key & Collectible Redemption Reminder
Trigger — Toki: member has an available Key or Collectible unredeemed for 7+ days
Softer than the expiry warning — this fires when a member has earned a reward but hasn't claimed it yet. Shows them exactly what's waiting, makes the redemption feel easy, and uses their current chapter to frame why this reward matters at this point in their journey. Converts passive earners into active redeemers.
Redemption
💎
Redemption Confirmation & Re-Earn Prompt
Trigger — Toki: member redeems a Key or Collectible
Fires the moment a member redeems. Celebrates the action, confirms what they just claimed, shows their remaining Stave balance, and immediately frames the next earning opportunity — what they need to do to earn their next Key. Turns one-time redeemers into habitual redeemers. Habitual redeemers are the highest-LTV segment in any loyalty program.
Redemption
🏆
Chapter Advancement (Level-Up) Flow
Trigger — Toki: member advances to a new chapter tier
The centerpiece of the entire program. One flow, 8 branches — Wanderer through Unwritten. Each advancement triggers an SMS same-day (immediate, by chapter name) followed by an email within 24 hours that reveals what's newly unlocked at that specific tier, reinforces the chapter's narrative identity, and issues a CTA tied to that chapter's exact perks. A Rule Breaker gets a different email than a Rebel gets a different email than a Maverick. Always. This is what makes the Legendary Club feel real instead of like a points card.
Chapter
🎯
Approaching Next Chapter
Trigger — Toki: member is within a set Stave threshold of their next chapter tier
Fires when a member is close to leveling up — "You're X Staves away from becoming a Rule Breaker." Names the specific chapter they're approaching, teases what it unlocks, and gives a direct path to earning those remaining Staves (buy a bottle, complete a challenge, bring a friend). The most purchase-intent-generating flow in the suite after the welcome series.
Chapter
📨
Referral Sent
Trigger — Toki: member shares their referral link
Confirmation that fires when a member sends their referral link. Acknowledges the share, reminds them what they earn when the friend converts (Staves + chapter credit), and encourages them to follow up with their contact. Keeps the referral loop warm while the friend is deciding.
Referral
🤝
Referral Completed
Trigger — Toki: referred friend makes their first purchase
Fires to the referrer when their friend converts. Awards Staves, celebrates the moment, and reinforces the behavior — "bring another friend, earn again." Frames referral as part of the chapter journey, not just a one-off incentive. Compounds over time as higher-chapter members have more social proof to leverage.
Referral
🥂
Referred Friend Welcome
Trigger — Toki: new member joins via a referral link
A distinct onboarding path for members who arrived through a referral. Acknowledges who brought them in, gives them a warm intro to the Legendary Club and their starting Stave bonus, and fast-tracks them toward their first earn action. Different tone from the standard welcome series — warmer, more personal, built around the shared connection that brought them in.
Referral
📱
Wallet Pass Flow
Branch 1 — Pass issued: member eligible for Apple/Google Wallet pass · Branch 2 — Pass update: Stave balance or chapter changes on the pass
Two branches, one flow. Branch 1 fires when a member's Wallet Pass is created — drives installation with a direct deep-link and shows them what the pass does (earn Staves in the tasting room, show at events, always have their chapter status on hand). Branch 2 fires when their balance or tier updates on the pass — a push-style email or SMS that keeps the pass feeling alive. Critical for SFR's physical tasting room: this is the bridge between an in-person visit and the digital loyalty program.
Wallet Pass
🧊
Dormant Member Win-Back
Trigger — Toki: Legendary Club member, no purchase or engagement in 60–90 days
Not a generic "we miss you." This flow is chapter-aware throughout — it names the member's current tier, highlights what's changed since they went quiet (new releases, new challenges, upcoming events), and uses their unspent Stave balance as the re-entry hook. 3 emails + 1 SMS over 14 days. Members who've invested effort into a chapter don't need a discount to return. They need a reminder the story isn't finished.
