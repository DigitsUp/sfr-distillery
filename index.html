Loyalty-Specific Flows
🥾
Legendary Club Welcome Series
Trigger — New member joins (Wanderer status confirmed via Toki)
5-email + 1 SMS sequence over 10 days. Opens with the legend arc — the path, the promise, what it means to begin the journey. Introduces Stave earning mechanics, previews what's locked in higher chapters, and drives the first bottle purchase before intent fades. Each email has a purchase behavior exit. This is the single most important flow in the entire suite — and it fires before anything else.
Phase 1 · Priority
🏆
Chapter Level-Up Flow
Trigger — Toki event: member advances to a new chapter tier
One flow, 8 branches — one per chapter tier. Each advancement triggers a 2-touch sequence: an SMS on the day of advancement (immediate congratulations, chapter name called out), then an email within 24 hours that reveals what's newly unlocked, reinforces the narrative of that specific chapter, and issues a CTA tied to the chapter's exact perk set. A Rule Breaker gets a different email than a Maverick. Always. This is what makes the loyalty program feel real.
Phase 1 · Priority
🔑
Stave Milestone & Redemption Nudge
Trigger — Toki: member reaches 50%, 75%, 100% of next Stave threshold / 7 days unredeemed at 100%
Three-stage nudge sequence that converts earners into redeemers. At 50%: a progress check-in showing exactly how far they've come and what's waiting. At 75%: urgency build — "you're this close." At 100% unredeemed for 7+ days: a direct, single-CTA redemption prompt showing the specific reward available (bottle discount, experience, collectible). No filler. No padding. Just the nudge that converts passive Stave holders into purchasers.
Phase 1 · Priority
🥃
Limited Release Early Access Flow
Trigger — New batch release announced; Legendary Club members receive this sequence first, before public announcement
Three phases: Pre-launch (72 hrs before) — member-exclusive preview with bottle story, production notes, and a "mark your calendar" CTA. Drop-day — SMS fires first (8am), email within 2 hours with a direct purchase link and available stock signal. Post-launch (48 hrs after) — splits by purchaser vs. non-purchaser. Purchasers get a collector confirmation and chapter acknowledgment. Non-purchasers get a last-chance send with remaining inventory count. Built to fire for every batch release — Batch 003 and beyond.
Phase 1 · Priority
🎂
Member Anniversary Flow
Trigger — 1-year anniversary of Legendary Club join date
A single, carefully written email that acknowledges how far the member has come on the path. References their current chapter by name. Celebrates the year with a one-time anniversary offer — a Stave bonus, a bottle discount, or an exclusive tasting invitation. In our experience running loyalty programs across DTC brands, member anniversary sends consistently deliver the highest open rates and revenue per recipient of any campaign type. This one gets written with care.
Phase 2
💎
First Redemption Celebration Flow
Trigger — Toki event: member redeems a Key or Collectible for the first time
The single biggest loyalty behavior gap: members who earn rewards and never redeem them. When a member redeems their first Key or Collectible, a short 2-email sequence fires — acknowledging the redemption by name, reinforcing what it means for their chapter progress, and immediately prompting the next earning opportunity. The goal is to turn one-time redeemers into habitual redeemers — because habitual redeemers are SFR's highest-LTV customer segment.
Phase 2
🧊
Dormant Member Reactivation
Trigger — Legendary Club member, no purchase or email engagement in 90 days
Not a generic "we miss you" sequence. This flow is chapter-aware — it references where the member currently sits on the path, what's changed since they went quiet (new releases, new chapters opening, upcoming events), and uses their Stave balance as a specific hook to come back. 3 emails + 1 SMS, timed across 14 days. Members who've invested effort into a chapter don't need a discount to return — they need a reminder that the story isn't finished.
Phase 2
📍
Tasting Room & Event Follow-Up
Trigger — Toki event: member attends a tour, tasting, or private event at SFR Distillery
In-person visits are the highest-intent signal a member can give. After a visit, a 2-email sequence fires: a post-visit recap that delivers cocktail recipes from what they tasted that day (personalized to the visit type), followed 5 days later by a Stave award notification for attending, a push toward the next chapter milestone, and a booking invite for their next experience or for bringing a friend. Converts one-time visitors into the most loyal segment in the program.

13 Flows.
Every Touchpoint, Covered.

Beyond the loyalty engine, SFR needs the foundational retention infrastructure that recovers abandoned revenue, builds post-purchase LTV, and keeps the list healthy. These flows run in parallel with the loyalty system — and every flow is chapter-aware where member data is available.

Browse AbandonmentTriggered: product page view, no add-to-cart
Re-engages browsers before intent cools. Copy references the specific product viewed — tasting notes, craft story, and Legendary Club Stave earning on purchase. 2–3 emails over 48 hours. Suppressed for members currently inside a loyalty flow.
Phase 1
Cart AbandonmentTriggered: add-to-cart, no checkout started
3-touch recovery sequence. Product reminder with craft narrative, Legendary Club member testimonials as social proof, and a Stave earning reminder on touch 3. SMS layer on high-value SKUs (limited releases). Chapter-tier-aware copy if member data is available in Klaviyo profile.
Phase 1
Checkout AbandonmentTriggered: checkout started, no purchase completed
Highest-converting abandonment flow — these buyers got furthest down the funnel. 2-email urgency sequence reinforcing free shipping at $125, the specific Stave count they'd earn on this order, and one friction-removal CTA. Direct and fast.
Phase 1
Post-Purchase — First BottleTriggered: first-ever order placed
Celebrates the first pour. Confirms or prompts Legendary Club Wanderer enrollment. Delivers cocktail recipe content tied to their specific SKU. Introduces adjacent catalog spirits with pairing context. Plants the seed for Batch 003. 4 emails over 14 days with Stave balance inclusion throughout.
Phase 1
Post-Purchase — Repeat CustomerTriggered: 2nd purchase onward
Deepens the relationship. Acknowledges their loyalty and current chapter by name. Introduces unexplored catalog spirits with cocktail context. Includes current Stave balance with a "you're X Staves from your next reward" message. Prompts tour or tasting booking as a chapter-progression activity.
Phase 2
SunsetTriggered: no engagement in 180+ days
A graceful last-chance exit. References the chapter the member achieved before going quiet. One final offer with urgency, then a clean suppression to protect deliverability. Non-members get a standard sunset; Legendary Club members get a chapter-aware farewell that leaves the door open.
